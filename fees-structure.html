FEE STRUCTURE


Fee Structure

The information regarding the fee for the courses can be collected from the college. Fee will be as per norms of Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Technical University .


Hostel Fee

The student has to pay the Hostel fee separately per year, which includes charges of fooding & lodging (Excluding caution money). Fees once deposited will not be refunded except certain conditions. The hostel fee has to be deposited at the time of admission.


Refund of Fee

If the student wishes to leave in mid of the session, the student will be expected to deposit entire fee for that year before obtaining NOC. The fee and the other charges paid at the time of admission or during the courses are non refundable. However any security deposit the caution money will be refunded only after the completion of the course.
