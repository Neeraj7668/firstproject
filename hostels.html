AMBALIKA INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT & TECHNOLOGY

Nothing is more expensive than a missed opportunity.


Best Engineering College Lucknow – Ambalika Institute and Group was established to provide high quality professional education in the streams of Engineering and Management. The Trustee is currently managing Ambalika Construction Limited, Ambalika Chit fund Limited and Ambalika Farms & Resorts Ltd. The main lines of business of the promoters are Infrastructure Development activities, Building Construction, Machinery Rental, Transportation & Logistics, Earthwork, Mining and Crushing. The business is carried out by the group’s parent company Ambalika Construction Limited. The state of Uttar Pradesh has vast natural resources. But it is still behind in the field of Technical and Management Education in particular. This adversely affects the industrial growth and overall economy of the state. Therefore, there is an urgent need of expansion of management and technical education in the state.


ACCOMMODATION

Pursuing higher education is a tough task for young boys & girls. In most cases, it requires relocation to an entirely new setting away from home, and usually for the first time. We are, therefore, sensitive to the sentiments of our students and strive to provide a home away from home. This is also especially meaningful because only when students are living comfortably they will be able to learn effectively. We have separate hostels for girls and boys at AIMT. Resident students have comfortable rooms, electricity, water facilities and nutritious food – all in clean, peaceful and hygienic surroundings. In addition, we have taken special measures to ensure privacy and security for the girls residing in our premises.


Hostel Blocks

The Blocks have been aesthetically designed and are located in serene surroundings so as to give a feeling of being at home. We provide students with facilities for some interesting leisure-time activities. The Boys’ Hostel is named as Brij Narayan Misra Hostel and has a capacity of 300 Students. The Girls’ Hostel is named as Subhadra Devi Hostel and has a capacity of 100 students.


Rooms

Each hostel block at AIMT has a dining facility. The mess is the place where maximum students’ interaction takes place and many exemplary friendships are made. That is why; each mess is spacious and- has very comfortable dining furniture. At AIMT, it is the students who decide the menu according to which meals are prepared and served. Our mess staff ensures complete cleanliness, thereby ensuring that the food is prepared and served in a hygienic manner.


Ambulance & Medical facilities

Medical Facility –The Academy has physicians on its rolls to visit the AIMT campus regularly. They can be consulted at the Campus Dispensary. The institute is located close to major hospitals in Lucknow, for accessing emergency services if immediate medical attention is required. The College also has 24 hours Ambulance service to cater to the emergency medical needs of students. Telephone Facility


Internet & Wifi facility

Our hostel has a 10 Mbps internet connection, locally networked and connected through WI-FI via the central server available to the students for their project work and seminar presentations. This WI-FI is free for all the staff members and students of the college.
